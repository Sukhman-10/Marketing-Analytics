Overview

##Objective

##Methodology
###1. Data Collection & Structuring

Collected cross-channel performance data across:

CTR, CPC, Lead Conversion Rates

CAC, CLV, NPS

Likelihood of Repeat Purchase

Customer Retention & CVR

Touchpoints required for purchase

Monthly spend & breakeven metrics

###2. Analytical Modeling

Built a multi-layer Excel model using advanced functions:

SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS, INDEX-MATCH

Automated conversion, cost, and revenue formulas

Cohort-style customer retention and CLV modeling

Forecasting models for revenue and net income

Dashboard visualizations (charts, trend lines, KPI tables)

###3. Performance Comparison

Benchmarked each platform on:

Efficiency (CPC, CPL, CAC)

Effectiveness (lead quality, conversions)

Value (CLV, NPS, repeat purchase)

Long-term growth (organic followers, retention uplift)

##Key Insights & Outcomes
Channel Performance Highlights

Facebook Ads excelled in CLV, NPS, and likelihood of buying again.

AdWords produced cost-efficient clicks and stable lead generation.

LinkedIn Ads delivered strong performance in professional targeting.

Organic channels demonstrated long-term reach and high retention potential.

##Financial Impact

Optimized allocation can significantly lower CAC.

High-CLV channels can increase long-term profitability.

Revenue forecasting models revealed faster breakeven under optimized spend.

##Strategic Impact

The findings directly shape future marketing strategy by revealing:

Where to increase or reduce advertising budget

Which channels produce the best customers (not just the most leads)

How to optimize conversion flows across touchpoints

Where to invest for long-term retention and follower growth

How to structure the next 12 months of marketing with predictable ROI

This analysis empowers the client to make smarter, more profitable marketing decisions with confidence.
